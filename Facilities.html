About Milimani Academy

Our school is dedicated to fostering a vibrant and nurturing community that guides every student's journey through education and personal growth. Our approach is founded on five core values that shape our ethos and influence every aspect of our curriculum and culture:
  • Engaging Curriculum
  • Expert Teachers
  • Safe Environment
  • Fun and Adventure

Facilities

Overview.

Milimani Academy is nestled in a prime location in Kitengela Milimani Area with convenient access via the Nairobi-Namanga highway. Committed to fostering excellence in academics and extracurricular pursuits, our facilities include: Our programs

  • State-of-the-art computer labs – 6 in total
  • Cutting-edge science laboratories furnished with top-tier equipment – 9 labs available
  • Expansive grounds for cricket, hockey, and football, complemented by a dedicated basketball court
  • Three swimming pools for aquatic activities and training
  • Well-stocked school libraries catering to diverse academic interests
  • A computerized resource center facilitating online research for students
  • On-campus canteen services offering nutritious meals
  • Abundant parking space for the convenience of parents and visitors
  • Spacious classrooms furnished with ergonomic furniture, ensuring student comfort
  • Disability-friendly infrastructure with modern washroom facilities
  • Collaboration with esteemed external service providers to enhance children's learning experiences
