Why Milimani Junior Secondary

At Milimani Academy Junior Secondary, we foster critical thinking and creativity in young minds through interactive experiences. Our custom-built facility welcomes all families, catering to diverse backgrounds and talents. Serving children ages 2½ to 5½, our premier Junior Secondary offers comprehensive education via thoughtfully designed indoor and outdoor spaces. Our personalized teaching methods boost self-esteem, nurturing open-mindedness and independence in our students. Our playgroup classrooms, though casual in appearance, are meticulously structured with a variety of engaging materials. Teachers inspire curiosity, guiding children through art, building, drama, games, gardening, sensory activities, and physical play.
