Life at Milimani Academy

Overview.

Ask any parent or guardian why Milimani Academy, and the answer will consistently revolve around Academics and Discipline!
The majority of students start their journey at Milimani Academy from Playgroup in Kindergarten, progressing all the way to A Levels. Spending over a decade within the walls of Milimani Academy signifies one thing – their future is shaped by their experiences during this time. In the words of Aristotle, "Give me a child, and I will show you the man." We are committed to ensuring that during these foundational years, children at Milimani Academy embody the essence of our mission statement: 'Empowering students to become individuals that make a difference in the world.' This responsibility is approached with utmost care and attention.
Milimani Academy serves as a rich reservoir of resources, encompassing both human and capital, to cater to the needs of our students who evolve into young adults as they transition to higher education. It also serves as a fertile ground from which our students draw from a variety of extracurricular activities, including clubs, sports, and other engaging activities fostering psychomotor and affective development.
Life at Milimani Academy
Think of squash, rounders, tennis, badminton, football, netball, rugby, hockey, talent shows, swimming galas… the list is extensive. This diverse array provides a safe outlet for the rigorous cognitive activities in the classroom, enhancing attentiveness and alertness, qualities we deeply value in our learners. Interhouse and interclass competitions facilitate the acquisition of leadership, public speaking, critical thinking, teamwork, and problem-solving skills – essential components of 21st-century education demanded by the community.
Moreover, today's world seeks well-rounded individuals; thus, these co-curricular engagements offer a platform for students to showcase and develop their talents. We have witnessed students shine both nationally and internationally through these avenues, providing them with a competitive advantage when seeking admission to esteemed universities and colleges that value not only academic grades but also extracurricular achievements.
At Milimani Academy, we believe that Empathy, Collaboration, Perseverance, Creativity and Independence. is the cornerstone of Academic Excellence. Progressive pastoral care is at the heart of our approach. We implement a Comprehensive CBC (Competency-Based Curriculum) that focuses on nurturing students emotionally, psychologically, physically, mentally, and spiritually. This holistic curriculum equips students not only for their time at school but also for life beyond the classroom, addressing topics such as self-awareness, resilience, responsible media usage, and interpersonal relationships.
On the Academic front, Milimani Academy excels. We offer a diverse range of subjects catering to the interests of our students, spanning Languages, Mathematics, Sciences, Arts, and Creative and Performing Arts. These subjects are taught by a team of highly qualified and dedicated educators who understand the unique needs of 21st-century learners.
Therefore, for those seeking an educational institution that caters to every child's needs, unlocks opportunities for holistic growth, and propels them towards esteemed higher education institutions, thereby shaping them into responsible individuals capable of making a positive impact on the world, Milimani Academy is the answer.
We have stood the test of time, earning trust through our proven track record of excellence!
