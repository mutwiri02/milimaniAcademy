Digital Learning with Somaplus

Somaplus is an innovative application designed to revolutionize education by providing a comprehensive digital learning platform for students and parents.

With Somaplus, you can access a wide range of features and tools to enhance the learning experience and streamline administrative tasks. Here's how Somaplus can benefit you:

  • Convenient Fee Payments: Easily pay school fees through the Somaplus app, saving time and hassle for parents and ensuring seamless financial transactions.

  • Diaries: Access digital diaries to stay updated on assignments, deadlines, and important announcements, keeping track of your child's academic progress effortlessly.

  • Report Cards: View digital report cards directly on the app, providing instant access to your child's grades and performance evaluations anytime, anywhere.

  • Fee Statements Viewing: Review detailed fee statements conveniently within the Somaplus platform, allowing for transparent financial management and planning.

  • Interactive Quizzes: Engage in interactive quizzes and educational activities to reinforce learning and stimulate intellectual growth, making studying more engaging and effective.
