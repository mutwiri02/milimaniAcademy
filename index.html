  • What are the school hours?

    Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

  • What extracurricular activities do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, arts, music, and academic clubs. Students can explore their interests and talents outside of regular classes.

  • How do you ensure student safety on campus?

    Student safety is our top priority. We have security personnel on campus, conduct regular safety drills, and maintain a secure environment through strict access control measures.

  • What is your approach to student discipline?

    We believe in a fair and supportive approach to discipline that focuses on teaching and learning. Our disciplinary policies are designed to promote positive behavior and ensure a respectful learning environment for all students.
